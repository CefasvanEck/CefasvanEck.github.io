New Hitdetection System

One of my latest achievement in my game eninge: visual hit detection.

A big problem I see in 3D games like shooters is the hitdetection that isn't that accurate. You want to shoot through an opening but there is a invisable hitbox blocking your bullets.

With my method, the model that you see is the hitbox model and won't decrease your performance that much.

You can also make the hitbox area bigger horizontal and/or vertical and with one HitDetection Point you can have up to 60 different objects using one.

The size of this field can be as big as you want.
