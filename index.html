Holum Island is known for
its vulcanic activities

Heavily inspired by the King Kong 2005 movie and the Book, this island is also known for its hostile enviorment, and I am not only talking about Lava flows.

Here you see one of the Basalt Caves I created for the game.
Basalt Pillars are a known result of Lava flowing into the sea and this adds to the mystery of the island: they look like man-made structures, carved out of stone.
